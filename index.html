Our Story

Anaki Bar is a premier destination for cocktails and entertainment. Located in the heart of Pokhara, we offer a sophisticated atmosphere and a wide range of drinks to satisfy every palate.

  • 2023

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Anaki Bar was founded with a vision to create an exceptional dining and entertainment experience in Pokhara.

  • December 23, 2023

    Pre-Opening Ceremony

    We celebrated the soft-opening of Anaki Bar with friends, family, and local figures.

  • January 1, 2024

    New Year 2024

    Our first major event was a spectacular New Year's celebration that set the tone for our future.
